Binturongs (Arctictis binturong) are relatives of the civet, which live in South and South-East Asia. They live for around 20 years on average. They are in the order Carnivora, even though they mostly eat fruit. They can make more than one nois. They can make a chuckling noise, snarl, grunt, hiss and females sometimes purr. They have prehensile tails and can also climb trees very well. Binturongs walk like bears, in a flat footed, side to side type of walk.

Fun Facts About Binturongs

They smell like buttered popcorn

Even though it is also called the bearcat, the binturong is not related to a bear or a cat

A baby binturong is called a pup, kitten or a bearcat
