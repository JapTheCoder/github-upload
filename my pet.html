My Dog

Information

My favourite pet is called Rocky. Rocky is a Jack Russel- Yorkshire Terrier cross. He has a sister called Chloe, who belongs to my sister.

Rocky is a funny dog, and is sometimes very grumpy. Rocky and Chloe are almost 2 years old and their birthday is on February 19th. My parents got them for Melissa (my sister) and I from a breeder in Nairobi.He is the first dog I have ever owned, and his favourite person is my mum. Rocky will sometimes whine at my mums bedroom door on weekends until she wakes up.

Rocky likes attention, going to the beach, sausages and we think he likes coffee. Whenever Melissa tries to comb his hair, he tries to get the hairbrsuh from her.The reason Rocky is my favourite is because he always does something funny. He doesn't like baths, driving in the car, other dogs and cats.
